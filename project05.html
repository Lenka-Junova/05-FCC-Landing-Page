How do we work?

  • You will submit your e-mail address.
  • We will contact you with few intial questions about the design you look for.
  • We will send you approximate cost calculation based on your specifications.
  • We will work on any adjustments based on your feedback and send you final quote and design spec.
  • After you agree with the price and design, we will start manufacturing your umbrella.
  • We will notify you when your umbrella is finished.
  • Your new beautiful umbrella is shipped to you. We hope it will serve you well.

All our umbrellas are hand-crafted, cruelty-free and we strive to ensure that all people within the supply chain get a fair wage.
